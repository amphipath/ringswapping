Bug Report

In MapleStory, changing equipment is restricted when direct attacking skills are used. Attempting to do so produces an error message in the middle of the screen.

However, in MapleSEA exclusively, there are many non-direct attacking skills that disable equipment changing, even though they should not. The reason why they shouldn't is because in Korea MapleStory (KMS), the base game, these skills do not disable equipment changing. In MapleSEA, this erroneous behaviour manifests as a chatbox message alerting us that we cannot change equips for 5 seconds after these skills do damage. This message does not exist in KMS because this behaviour does not exist in KMS, and should be fixed.
